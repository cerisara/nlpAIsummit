## Frugality is social, and its absence is a problem

While algorithms and hardware keep getting more efficient, computing, and in
particular AI, keeps requiring more power. AI research has embedded in its
current dominant narrative tales of a "bitter lessson", where bigger alway
wins. I will discuss how the importance of size is a simplied narrative,
leaving out crucial mathematical and algorithmic ingredients, also leaving out
tasks and application. I will also explore the societal and political economy
mechanisms and consequence of our current race to scale, as summarized in our
paper on the "Price of the Bigger-is-Better Paradigm in AI".
